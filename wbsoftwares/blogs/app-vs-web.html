App vs Website: which one is better?

A mobile app will always be for a particular platform and it will not have as much reach as a website. But still here are some good points to consider.

An app gives you branding opportunities as it is totally separate from the company's website. It can have a new offer data app exclusively.An app is like a personally tailored cloth, it is highly customisable to your brand liking and it will help you reach your target audience.

As it is platform based it can take advantage of the platform and the device such as mobiles and tablets.Sometimes it will give you offline accessibility which is a huge deal in customer service.An app highly increases the customer engagement rate as you can communicate with your customers directly.

A website gives you a huge platform to reach a new target audience and it is cheaper as well. Here are some good points to consider. It is highly likely that your customers will find your business by searching on the web as our website is a cross platform product which can reach a broader audience.

Depending upon the complexity of your website it can be cost effective for your business as well. Search engine optimization will highly increase your brand visibility on a broader scale which is unique to websites only. when your business will rank higher on the search result then there is a higher possibility that your business will reach a lot of new customers.
